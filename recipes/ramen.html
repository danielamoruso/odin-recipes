Ramen

Description

This is a delicious and hearty ramen recipe that's perfect for any occasion.

Ingredients

  • Ramen noodles
  • Chicken broth
  • Soft-boiled eggs
  • Green onions
  • Shiitake mushrooms

Steps

  1. Cook the ramen noodles according to package instructions.
  2. Heat the chicken broth in a large pot.
  3. Add the soft-boiled eggs, green onions, and shiitake mushrooms to the pot.
  4. Combine the cooked noodles with the broth mixture and serve hot.
