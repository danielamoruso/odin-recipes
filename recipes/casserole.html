Casserole

Description

This is a delicious and hearty casserole recipe that's perfect for any occasion.

Ingredients

  • Ground beef
  • Tomato sauce
  • Mozzarella cheese
  • Onions
  • Garlic

Steps

  1. Preheat the oven to 375°F (190°C).
  2. In a large skillet, brown the ground beef and add the tomato sauce.
  3. Add the onions and garlic to the skillet and cook until softened.
  4. Transfer the mixture to a baking dish and top with mozzarella cheese.
  5. Bake for 25-30 minutes, or until the cheese is melted and bubbly.
