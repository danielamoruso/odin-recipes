Lasagna

Description

This is a delicious and hearty lasagna recipe that's perfect for any occasion.

Ingredients

  • Lasagna noodles
  • Ground beef
  • Tomato sauce
  • Mozzarella cheese

Steps

  1. Cook the lasagna noodles according to package instructions.
  2. Preheat the oven to 375°F (190°C).
  3. In a large skillet, brown the ground beef and add the tomato sauce.
  4. Layer the lasagna noodles, meat sauce, and mozzarella cheese in a baking dish.
  5. Bake for 25-30 minutes, or until the cheese is melted and bubbly.
